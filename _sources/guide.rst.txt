Instructor's guide
------------------



Preparing to Teach
------------------

    - Making sure that all the compilers are installed correctly on the system 
    - Run all the examples beforehand at least once
    - Be aware which parts could be skipped in case needed
    - Give enough time for the exercises
    - Do not open too many tabs and switch among them
    - Emphasize the differences between C/C++ and Fortran for certrain directives
    - Briefly introduce the exercises before and make a short summary afterwards on the most important take-home messages
